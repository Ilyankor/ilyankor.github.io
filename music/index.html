Music

This page will be about my music. It will contain my compositions, with links to audio and scores where available.

List of compositions

Spin One-Half, 4" (2021)
piano

A piece for David Furney. Part of a collaboration bewteen the composition studios and Dr. Staupe's piano studio at the University of Houston in the fall of 2021.


Marbles in a bag, 13" (2021)
two cellos

Experiments in using the pentatonic collection and probabilities determining aspects of pitch and rhythm.


Alarm, 7" (2021)
flute, clarinet, violin, cello, piano, percussion

A rondo in which the theme is a ringing alarm. However, the sleeping person is able to snooze it for quite a while and enjoys dream episodes in between.


Sonata in B-flat, 3" (2020)
violin, piano

A short piece in sonata form created to show a friend that I can write in the style of common practice music.


Strive, 5" (2020)
cello

A loose sonata form in minor mode obtains resolution in the major mode.


Hexenjagd, 7" (2020)
wind quintet

An innocent girl lives a carefree life when she stumbles into an enchanted forest. She encounters magic and returns home changed, but in fear and disgust, the townspeople bring her to trial for witchcraft.


Beating eggs, 3" (2020)
alto saxophone, piano

A fast-paced piece featuring the octatonic collection.


Off the Path, 6" (2019)
alto saxophone, piano

Two adventurers in a video game unfortunately go where they should not have and face the consequences of their misstep.


Shifting equilibria, 3" (2019)
double bass, trombone

An exercise in intervallic tension.
