ABOUT

Miles Bi is a third-year undergraduate student at the University of Houston, studying both math and music. His music looks to innovate using abstract principles and detailed oriented processes, sometimes involving mathematics. He studies composition with Rob Smith and viola with Suzanne LeFevre. In his free time, he enjoys learning new things and taking walks in the sun.
